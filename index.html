What I believe about retail media
On-site sponsored products are table stakes. The real growth is in off-site, in-store, and CTV — and most retailers aren't ready for it.
LATAM is 3–4 years behind the US in retail media maturity, but the velocity is faster. First movers will own the market.
Fintech ad networks — super-apps, neobanks, loyalty platforms — are the next wave. Same playbook, better data.
Owning your ad stack isn't a nice-to-have — it's the difference between 30% margins and 80% margins on every ad dollar.
